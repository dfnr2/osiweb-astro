TOSIE Printout was published by The Ohio Scientific Information Exchange (TOSIE), a user group focused on OSI computer systems. The newsletter provided technical articles, hardware modifications, software tips, and community information for OSI enthusiasts.

*Many thanks to Steve Gray for these scans!*
